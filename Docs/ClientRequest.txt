The client wants a system that uses AI to improve their communication skills.

Features required:
1. Jumble Sentence:- 
The AI will generate and jumble up the sequence of a sentence, the user is required to restructure the sentence.
Based on the result, user should get a score.

2. Speech Check:-
The AI will say a sentence, and the user needs to say it back, based on that give a score of accuracy.

3. Story Summary:- 
The AI will generate a story and tell it via voice, and the user needs to summaries or explain it. 
Based on the explaination give a score.
